…in most tech companies, these roles [engineer, developer, programmer]—much more than the designers, copywriters, marketers, and others who work on the creative or “soft skills” end of the spectrum—are seen as the masterminds of new technology. They’re paid the best, recruited the hardest, and often have the most power on teams. While you’re likely to find the staff is a bit more diverse outside the technical roles—and in particular, that women are better represented in communications-related jobs—those roles are historically undervalued (which is a whole other problem in tech culture…)
